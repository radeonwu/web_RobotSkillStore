Stage 3 of 4 · Lifecycle

What happens

  • Governance snapshot — the platform evaluates success rate, violation rate, and regression signals over a rolling run window.
  • Evidence tiers — more runs unlock broader deployment permissions and higher trust signals for users.
  • Status promoted to CERTIFIED — the governance snapshot is published alongside the skill, visible in the Evidence Viewer.

What the platform does

  • Computes the governance snapshot via GET /skills/{skill_id}/{version}/governance.
  • Issues certification when all policy thresholds are met — no manual approval required.
  • Automatically triggers DEGRADED if a previously certified skill's evidence drops below thresholds.
