RSS Safety Summary

Safety is enforced by the platform at runtime via non-bypassable policy gates.

Policy gates (examples)
  • Approval: who can run what, where, and when.
  • Capability bounds: speed/force limits, workspace constraints.
  • Environment checks: fixtures, sensors, interlocks.
  • Evidence requirement: mandatory artifacts for audit.
