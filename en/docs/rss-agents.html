RSS Agents Summary

Agents are an internal decision layer in RSS. They propose Runs; RSS enforces policies and records evidence.

Clean separation
  • Agent: decides “what to try next”.
  • RSS Platform: decides “what is allowed to happen” (policy gates).
  • RSP: defines objects/invariants so results are auditable and portable.
