Why Determinism Matters

In the physical world, uncertainty is expensive. Determinism is a safety and scalability requirement.

Physical failure cost is high

Robot actions can damage hardware, harm people, or stop production. RSS treats determinism as a prerequisite for certification and deployment.

Determinism enables governance

Constraints, evidence, and repeatable runs make it possible to compare versions, enforce gates, and provide compatibility statements.

Agents need reliable primitives

Agents can decide what to do, but they need stable primitives to execute safely. RSS skills provide those primitives.
