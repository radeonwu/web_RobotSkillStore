Protocol hooks (illustrative)

Concrete schemas can evolve, but these hooks define stable integration points for implementations and the store.

Skill.register(manifest, endpoint, verification_artifacts) Skill.discover(query={capability, constraints, domain, trust_level}) Skill.call(name, inputs, mode="sync|async|stream", timeout_s, policy={safety,resources}) Telemetry.subscribe(skill_run_id)
