Skill Lifecycle

How RSS turns robot capabilities into trusted, reusable assets: Register → Validate → Certify → Deploy.

Lifecycle

Overview

  • Register: publish skill spec — interface, constraints, error schema.
  • Validate: submit execution runs; RSS indexes results and artifacts.
  • Certify: governance snapshot evaluates evidence and issues certification.
  • Deploy: users reuse certified skills with predictable, auditable behavior.
