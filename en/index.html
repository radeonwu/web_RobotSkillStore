Why RSS matters

Today, robot capabilities are highly fragmented. "Skills" are often buried in code, tightly coupled to specific robots and environments, and hard to reuse.

This fragmentation leads to poor sharing, unexpressed safety constraints, and sim-to-real transfer that doesn't scale.

Robot Skill Store is designed to solve this root problem.
