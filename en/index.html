What we’re building

A full loop: Author → Validate → Publish → Discover → Invoke → Monitor → Improve. This enables a skill library that grows over time, across robots and deployments.

  • Skill SDK for authoring & testing
  • Skill Runtime for safe execution
  • Skill Store for discovery & distribution
  • Agent Orchestration for composition & recovery
Why Agent*Skill?
Agents should not “drive the robot.” They should invoke skills that are bounded, monitored, and safety-guarded. This makes scaling operationally realistic.
